Tobi The Pembroke Welsh Corgi

Tobi is a 2 year old Pembroke Welsh Corgi, he loves socks, walks, eating ice and almost everything else he can get his teeth into. Tobi is a little over weight but he is now on a diet because if corgi's are overweight, it is not healthy for them as they get older. This is because there is a common health problem for corgi's if they are overweight they can develop something called hip dysplasia. What this is, is that the hip joint grinds a long the hip instead of smoothly sliding.
