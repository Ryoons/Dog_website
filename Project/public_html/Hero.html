Hero The Shiba Inu

Hero is a rare Shiba Inu, most shiba inus are either Black or Brown. Shiba Inus are known for their personality, Hero is no different he has a very sassy attitude and eats only the best food. He does not eat kibble anymore, his meals consist of rice, chicken, vegetable and a chicken broth. Hero is 8 years old and loves ordering around his humans. He gets walked 4 times a day and gets to choose where he wants to walk to every day. Hero is a princess.
